Namespace Roles

A "role" is an individual participant in a signing flow, such as a signer or CC contact. Roles are identified by their names, which must be unique (e.g. 'Recipient 1'). Template fields are assigned to roles for signing operations, so you may have 'Recipient 1 Signature 1' and so forth.
